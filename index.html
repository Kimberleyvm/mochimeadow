Our Kawaii Menu

Explore our delightful selection of Kawaii-inspired dishes!

Starters

Begin your kawaii journey with these adorable and delicious appetizers.


  1. Panda Paws Dumplings

    Steamed dumplings shaped like panda paws, filled with vegetables and tofu.

  2. Sakura Blossom Salad

    A fresh salad with edible flowers, avocado, and a sesame dressing.

  3. Bunny Hop Croquettes

    Golden potato croquettes, served with a carrot dip.

  4. Chibi Chicken Bites

    Crispy heart-shaped chicken nuggets, served with teriyaki sauce.

  5. Kitty Katsu Skewers

    Mini chicken katsu skewers with sweet chili sauce.

Main Dishes

Indulge in hearty and colorful main courses, crafted to make you smile.

  1. Kawaii Katsu Curry

    A rice dish with homemade katsu curry, served with a cute rice bear.

  2. Totoros Bento Box

    A bento box with sushi, tempura, and steamed veggies, garnished with nori faces.

  3. Sakura Sushi Rolls

    Sushi rolls filled with salmon, avocado, and cucumber, decorated like cherry blossoms.

  4. Magical Miso Don

    A rice bowl with miso-glazed eggplant and sesame, topped with edamame and spring onions.

  5. Kirbys Kawaii Burgers

    Mini burgers in pink buns with lettuce, cheese, and teriyaki sauce.

Desserts

End on a sweet note with our playful, kawaii-inspired desserts!

  1. Fluffy Marshmallow Mochi

    Mochi filled with marshmallow cream, decorated with rainbow sprinkles.

  2. Totoro Taiyaki

    Taiyaki fish-shaped waffles filled with sweet red bean paste or Nutella.

  3. Rainbow Parfait

    Layered ice cream with fruit, granola, and a sugar cloud on top.

  4. Kitty Cupcake Platter

    Three cute mini cupcakes decorated as cats, with various flavors.

  5. Unicorn Cheesecake

    Mini cheesecakes in pastel colors, topped with an edible unicorn horn.

Kawaii Freakshakes

Our over-the-top, Instagram-worthy freakshakes are as fun as they are delicious.

  1. Bubblegum Bunny Blast

    A pastel blue milkshake with bubblegum flavor, topped with a large pink cotton candy bunny and marshmallows.

  2. Strawberry Mochi Madness

    Strawberry shake with mochi balls, whipped cream, a strawberry macaron, and a small mochi ice bear on the rim.

  3. Unicorn Dream Shake

    Rainbow vanilla milkshake with unicorn sprinkles, cotton candy, a mini donut, and a rainbow heart.

  4. Panda Cookie Crunch

    Chocolate cookie milkshake, topped with a panda ice cream, Oreo cookies, and chocolate sauce.

  5. Tropical Totoro Twist

    Coconut-pineapple milkshake, topped with a small Totoro made of fondant, tropical fruit, and coconut flakes.
